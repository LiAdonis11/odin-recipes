Basic Baked Spaghetti

Easy baked spaghetti made quickly in the oven. Use different cheeses to create your signature dish.

Ingredients

  • Â¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup shredded mild Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step

    3. Cook beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat until crumbly and brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Stir spaghetti sauce into beef. Reduce heat and simmer.

  3. Step

    4. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Stir in spaghetti; cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.

  4. Step

    5. Add spaghetti to meat mixture; mix well. Transfer to a 9x13-inch dish. Top with Cheddar cheese.

  5. Step

    6. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheese is bubbly, about 30 minutes.
